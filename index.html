About this dataset

This is the open, machine-readable form of the Heavenly Calendar used by the Family Federation for World Peace and Unification. All dates are kept as lunar_md strings ("M.D" format), with two solar–lunar anchors for verification and a deterministic ahn_shil_il_rule for the weekly day of rest.
